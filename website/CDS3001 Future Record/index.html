Notes and references
Named after 'Blade Runner 2049', a cyberpunk sci-fi film based in a highly technological yet dystopian future. In particular I likened the idea of digital clothes to character and avatar customisation, such as the android characters in Blade Runner, allowing full customisation of features and clothing.
