About

hello! my name is Nicole Leaw and i am a multidisciplinary designer, with skills spanning from website design, coding, animation, video editing, 3d art, communication design and 2d illustration.

i graduated with a double degree in Design and Information Technology, majoring in Communication Design and Interactive Media. thus i am very passionate about skills that allow me to combine both of these fields! ✧˖°
